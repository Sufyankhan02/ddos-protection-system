About This Website

This website is intentionally vulnerable to DDoS attacks for demonstration purposes. It has no protection mechanisms in place.

In the next part of our project, we'll show how to protect against these attacks by analyzing human behavior patterns.

What is a DDoS Attack?

A Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt normal traffic of a targeted server, service or network by overwhelming the target or its surrounding infrastructure with a flood of Internet traffic.

In our demonstration, we'll use the Slowloris attack which works by holding as many connections to the target web server open for as long as possible.
